About

Frank C. Church is a Professor in the Departments of Pathology and Laboratory Medicine, Pharmacology, and Medicine in the School of Medicine at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. He received his BS (1975) and MS (1978) degrees from Louisiana State University; he received a PhD (1982) from North Carolina State University; and after a postdoctoral fellowship at UNC-CH, has been on the faculty at UNC-CH since 1986.
