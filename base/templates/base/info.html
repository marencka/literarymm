What is Literary Mindfulness Meditation?


Literary Mindfulness Meditation is reading something and entering the mindset of what you have read.

You should achieve a temporary but beneficial calmness.

We are living in a world of anxiety, tension, and moments of negativity...

Using Literary Mindfulness Meditation will help releasing this anxiousness and promote resiliency.

What is Active Reflection?


Active Reflection is focusing your mindset for a moment and then writing your thoughts about the provided reading(s).

The mindfulness journey begins by reading, pausing to think and ends by actively reflecting on your thoughts in writing a response. You should achieve a temporary but beneficial calmness.
