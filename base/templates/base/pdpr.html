Parkinson's Disease Progress Report (PDPR)

PDPR is a balance sheet created to give a weekly mental health score for someone with Parkinson's disease and/or their Care Partner(s). The PDPR will be a 15-question survey about Quality-of-Life (QoL). Stress, life-work-exercise balance, and overall wellness will be monitored.

Please answer the following questions with regards to the past week (using a Likert scalee scoring system):

Never - 1

Occasionally - 2

Sometimes - 3

Often - 4

Always - 5
