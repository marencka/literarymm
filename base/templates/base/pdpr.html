PDPR

Parkinson's Disease Progress Report (PDPR) is a balance sheet created to give a weekly mental health score for someone with Parkinson's disease and/or their Care Partner(s) read. The PDPR will be a 15-question survey about Quality-of-Life (QoL). Stress, life, work, exercise, balance, and overall wellness will be monitored.

Please answer the following questions with regards to the past week (using a Likert scale scoring system):

1(Never) 2(Occasionally) 3(Sometimes) 4(Often) 5(Always)
