PDPR

Parkinson's Disease Progress Report (PDPR) is a balance sheet created to give a weekly mental health score for someone with Parkinson's disease and/or their Care Partner(s) read. The PDPR will be a 15-question survey about Quality-of-Life (QoL). Stress, life, work, exercise, balance, and overall wellness will be monitored.

Please answer the following questions with regards to the past week (using a Likert scale scoring system):

1(Never) 2(Occasionally) 3(Sometimes) 4(Often) 5(Always)


Question 1: Had difficulty functioning at work?

Question 2: Had problems exercising?

Question 3: Had problems sleeping?

Question 4: Had difficulty with daily personal cleaning/grooming and getting dressed?

Question 5: Had problems preparing food and eating meals?

Question 6: Had problems concentrating at work or at home watching TV?

Question 7: Had problems communicating with others?

Question 8: Had pain in body, joints, or muscles?

Question 9: Felt sad and depressed?

Question 10: Felt alone and isolated?

Question 11: Felt anxious?

Question 12: Felt embarrassed to be seen or go out in public?

Question 13: Felt a lack of support from loved ones or close friends?

Question 14: Felt un-balanced (collectively) in life-work-family-living with Parkinson's disease?

Question 15: Felt unable to manage (overall) daily life-challenge(s) from Parkinson's disease?
